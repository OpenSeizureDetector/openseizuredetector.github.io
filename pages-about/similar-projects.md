# Similar Projects

There are a few other projects that have similar goals to OpenSeizureDetector that have started up while I have been working on this, which readers may like to look into.

I have put the information I have on the other projects into a [comparison table](http://openseizuredetector.org.uk/static/Similar_Projects.html).

If you know of other similar projects that should be included in this list, please email graham@openseizuredetector.org.uk.
