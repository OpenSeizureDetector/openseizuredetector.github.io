Free, open source tools to alert carers when someone may be having a seizure.

What is OpenSeizureDetector?

OpenSeizureDetector uses a smartwatch and an Android phone to monitor movement and heart rate to detect possible tonic-clonic seizures and alert carers.

It is designed to work on low-cost hardware so that it is accessible to as many people as possible.

How it Works

  1. A smartwatch measures movement and heart rate
  2. An Android phone analyses this data
  3. An alert is raised if patterns suggest a seizure
